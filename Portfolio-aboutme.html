Hey there!

I am a UX-designer and front-end developer from and based in Stockholm. I love to be creative through dance - currently moving through life with salsa steps. I also enjoy photography, interior design and would love to be able to make my own clothes and put my grandma's old sewing machine to use!

Whenever I get the chance I travel to experience a new adventure (preferably in a van next to the beach), or I'll spend my time listening to a podcast about the human mind and behaviour. My inner artist and hobby-psychologist go hand in hand in my work as a UX-designer, with frontend-knowledge as the icing on the cake.

What excites me most is the human-centered approach to solving problems, simplifying everyday life. It’s about crafting intuitive experiences that guide users effortlessly, just like the flow and well-timed rhythm in dance. To create a user journey they'll want to tell all their friends about.
